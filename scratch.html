Reasons why Lamar Jackson is the GOAT

  1. Record-Breaking Stats, MVPs, and Career Accomplishments
    • 2x NFL MVP (2019 & 2023) - including a unaminous win in 2019 (only the second player in history to do so)
    • Youngest QB to ever win MVP at just 22 years old
    • First and only QB with multiple 1,000-yard rushing seasons
    • Holds the all-time single-season QB rushing record (1,206 yards)
    • Fastest player ever to 5,000+ passing and 2,500+ rushing yards
    • Top 5 all-time win percentage among QBs with 50+ starts
    • Won the Heisman Trophy in college (2016) at just 19 years old—becoming one of the most electric players in NCAA history

  2. He Changed the Game Forever
    • Teams now build their entire systems around dual-threat QBs
    • Young stars like Jalen Hurts, Anthony Richardson, and Malik Willis owe part of their shot to the trail Lamar blazed
    • He forced defensive coordinators to change their schemes, and general managers to rethink how to scout the position

  3. Elite IQ, Clutch Gene, and Leadership
    • His master of complex RPOs and option reads shows elite football IQ
    • Has engineered multiple comeback wins, including a 19-point 2nd half rally against the Colts in 2021 where he posted 442 yards and 4 TDs
    • In 2023, he led the Ravens to the #1 seed, dominating with a new OC and system, proving he's coachable and adaptable
    • Teammates describe him as humble, focused, and inspiring
    • He elevates everyone around him—even when the roster is thin
